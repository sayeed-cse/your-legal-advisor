Education Law

The field of education law focuses on laws and regulations that govern federal and state schools and school boards and school systems, as well as protecting students' rights. It originally developed as a legal specialization to ensure all students, faculty and staff within an educational system are treated fairly and that their civil rights are not compromised.

Tax Law

Tax law or revenue law is an area of legal study in which public or sanctioned authorities, such as federal, state and municipal governments (as in the case of the US) use a body of rules and procedures (laws) to assess and collect taxes in a legal context.
